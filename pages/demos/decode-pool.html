Decode pool — scrubbing a photo timeline on a canvas

Sixteen frames, rendered to a canvas as you drag the slider. The left panel calls drawImage on an <img> whose src is set on every scrub — the browser decodes on demand, on the main thread. The right panel warms a DecodePool, then draws from cached ImageBitmaps — every frame is a single blit. Watch the frame time reading while you drag quickly.
