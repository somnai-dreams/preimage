Predictive pre-rendering · baselines

Phase 0 for the predictive-pre-rendering swing. Runs a scripted scroll trajectory (constant / accelerating / decelerating / direction-change / fling) and evaluates three predictors: stationary (always predicts current), linear (velocity × horizon), momentum (velocity with exponential drag). Reports prediction error distribution at multiple horizons so we can compare "cheap physics beats fixed overscan?" before committing to an ML model.

The bench doesn't drive a tile pool — it captures raw scroll samples and evaluates predictors against ground truth. Tolerance = treat a prediction as "hit" if it's within ±overscan of actual.
